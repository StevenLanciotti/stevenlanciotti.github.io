This tool fetches 8‑K filings from the SEC EDGAR database and runs NLP-based sentiment analysis to reveal linguistic signals.  
It also charts stock performance around filing dates for easy insight.

**🛠 Built With:** Python, pandas, BeautifulSoup, yFinance
